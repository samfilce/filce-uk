## Our Process

1. **Contact** — Email us at [contact@filce.uk](mailto:contact@filce.uk)
2. **Discussion** — We'll discuss your product and timeline
3. **Review** — Hands-on testing (2-4 weeks typical)
4. **Draft** — We'll share the review for factual accuracy
5. **Publish** — Live on filce.uk with affiliate links
6. **Promotion** — Social sharing as agreed

## Review Policy

- We **do not** accept payment for positive reviews
- We **do** accept review units (kept or returned as agreed)
- We **always** disclose if a product was provided
- We **will** mention flaws honestly
- We **won't** review products we can't genuinely recommend
