# Submit a Product for Review

Got a product that belongs on filce.uk? We'd love to hear from you.

## What We Review

We specialise in practical, hands-on reviews for:

- **Home Automation** — Smart devices, sensors, Home Assistant integrations
- **Networking** — Routers, access points, switches, cameras
- **Tools & DIY** — Workshop equipment, hobbyist tools
- **Tech Accessories** — Phone cases, chargers, cables, gadgets
- **Pet Tech** — GPS trackers, cameras, feeders, smart collars
- **Kitchen & Home** — Practical gadgets for home life

## Our Review Style

We don't do surface-level unboxings. Our reviews include:

- ✅ Real product photos (not press images)
- ✅ Hands-on testing over days/weeks
- ✅ Practical use cases from a real home
- ✅ Honest pros and cons
- ✅ SEO-optimised for discoverability
- ✅ Affiliate purchase links

## Before You Contact Us

**We need:**
- A UK-relevant product (shipping to UK)
- A product that fits our categories
- Genuine confidence in your product quality

**We don't accept:**
- Paid positive reviews
- Products we can't test properly
- Products outside our expertise
- Vape, CBD, gambling, or adult products

## How to Submit

**Email us at:** [contact@filce.uk](mailto:contact@filce.uk)

**Include:**
1. Product name and category
2. Brief description
3. UK retail price
4. Your website/product page URL
5. Whether you can provide a review unit
6. Any timeline requirements

## What Happens Next

1. **Review** — We'll assess fit within 48 hours
2. **Confirm** — If accepted, we'll arrange delivery
3. **Test** — 2-4 weeks of hands-on use
4. **Draft** — We'll share for factual accuracy (not editorial control)
5. **Publish** — Live on filce.uk
6. **Share** — Social promotion (optional, by agreement)

## Review Unit Policy

- **Keep or Return:** We'll discuss what works for you
- **Disclosure:** We'll clearly state if a product was provided
- **Honesty:** We won't guarantee a positive review
- **Timeline:** We'll agree on a realistic deadline upfront

## Questions?

Email [contact@filce.uk](mailto:contact@filce.uk) — we respond within 48-72 hours.
