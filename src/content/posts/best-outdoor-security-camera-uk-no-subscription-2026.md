## Why Avoid Subscription Cameras?

Before the picks, it's worth understanding what you're opting out of.

**Ring Basic:** £3.49/month per camera (or £8/month for unlimited). No subscription = no event history, no video sharing, no person detection.

**Google Nest Aware:** £6/month for 30-day history. Without it, you only get the last three hours on some cameras.

**Arlo Secure:** From £2.99/camera/month or £12.99/month for unlimited. Without it, many features are locked.

Over three years with five cameras, that's **£500–£2,000 in subscription fees** — on top of the hardware cost. Cameras that store locally cost nothing after the initial purchase.

## PoE vs WiFi vs Battery — Which Is Right for You?

| | PoE | WiFi | Battery/Solar |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Reliability** | ★★★★★ | ★★★☆☆ | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Setup difficulty** | Medium (cabling) | Easy | Very easy |
| **Recording** | 24/7 continuous | 24/7 (on good WiFi) | Motion clips only |
| **Power cost** | ~£0 (cable once) | ~£0 | £0 (solar) |
| **Best for** | Permanent installs | Rentals, flexibility | Sheds, no cable routes |

**My recommendation:** If you own your home and can run Ethernet, go PoE. It's more work upfront but dramatically more reliable than WiFi or battery. If you can't run cables, WiFi + NAS is a solid middle ground.

## FAQ

### Do I need internet for these cameras to work?

No. PoE cameras connected to a local NVR record entirely on your local network. You don't need internet access for recording to work. You need internet only if you want remote viewing via the manufacturer's app.

### What is PoE and do I need a special router?

PoE (Power over Ethernet) delivers electrical power alongside data through a standard Ethernet cable. You don't need a special router — you need a PoE switch or a PoE-capable NVR. See our [Best PoE Switches guide](/posts/best-poe-switches-uk-2026/) for options.

### Can these cameras work with Home Assistant?

Yes. All PoE cameras in this guide support RTSP streams and most are ONVIF compliant, making them fully compatible with Home Assistant. Reolink cameras have a native Home Assistant integration. For a full setup guide, see [Home Assistant pet monitoring setup →](/posts/home-assistant-pet-monitoring/).

### How much storage do I need?

A 4-camera system recording 24/7 at 4K uses roughly 1–2TB per month. A 2TB HDD will hold 2–4 weeks of footage before overwriting the oldest recordings. Most NVRs handle this automatically.

### Is Reolink worth it for UK buyers?

Yes. Reolink offers excellent value in the UK, with cameras available on Amazon Prime and solid warranty support. Read our detailed [Reolink vs Hikvision UK comparison →](/posts/reolink-vs-hikvision-uk-2026/) if you're choosing between brands.

### What's the best outdoor security camera for renters?

If you can't drill or run cables, the TP-Link Tapo C320WS (WiFi, microSD local storage) or Reolink Argus 3 Pro (battery/solar, no cables needed) are the best subscription-free options for renters.
