## What You Actually Need to Monitor Your Dog

Before spending money, decide what level of monitoring you want:

| Level | What You Get | Cost |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **Basic** | Phone app camera, live view | £30–£80 |
| **Intermediate** | PoE camera, local recording, alerts | £80–£200 |
| **Advanced** | Home Assistant, AI detection, automations | £200–£500+ |

Most people are happy at the intermediate level. If you already run a home server or NAS, the advanced setup pays for itself quickly.

## H2: FAQ — Dog Monitoring at Home

### What is the best camera to monitor a dog at home in the UK?

A PoE fisheye camera gives the best coverage for most rooms. The Reolink Fisheye is our top pick — it covers an entire room from one mount, works with Home Assistant, and has two-way audio. For a Wi-Fi option with easier setup, Reolink's E1 Outdoor or TP-Link Tapo cameras are solid budget picks.

### Can I talk to my dog through a camera?

Yes — any camera with two-way audio lets you speak through your phone. It can calm an anxious dog, interrupt destructive behaviour, or just reassure them you're coming back. The Reolink Fisheye supports this natively.

### How do I get alerts when my dog is moving around?

Basic cameras send motion alerts via their app. For smarter alerts — like knowing your dog has left the sofa or entered the kitchen — Home Assistant with Frigate (an AI object detection add-on) lets you set up person/pet detection alerts that ignore swaying curtains and passing cars.

### Is a PoE camera better than Wi-Fi for dog monitoring?

PoE (Power over Ethernet) cameras are more reliable — they're wired, so no Wi-Fi dropouts, no batteries, and no reconnection issues. If you're away from home and the camera drops offline, you've lost your monitoring. PoE is worth the cable run for peace of mind.

### How much does a proper dog monitoring setup cost?

A basic Wi-Fi camera: £30–£60. A PoE camera with local recording: £80–£150. A full Home Assistant setup with NVR, multiple cameras, and automations: £300–£600+. Most people get 90% of the value from a single good camera plus a free app.

### Can Home Assistant monitor my dog automatically?

Yes. With Frigate AI detection, Home Assistant can alert you when it detects a dog in a specific zone, when motion continues for more than X minutes (potential anxiety), or when your dog is near restricted areas. You can also automate lights and music to switch on when you leave, which helps with separation anxiety.
