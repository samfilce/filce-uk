## How to Choose the Right Robot Vacuum

### Do you have pets?
Pet hair wraps around brush rolls and clogs filters fast. Look for **rubber dual-brush systems** (Roborock and Eufy) rather than bristle brushes. Higher suction (5,000Pa+) also helps pull embedded hair from carpet.

### Do you have hard floors, carpet, or both?
If you have mixed flooring, get a model with **automatic carpet detection** and mop-lift. Running a wet mop over carpet is a quick way to ruin both.

### How big is your home?
Entry-level models use gyro or random navigation — fine for a flat, frustrating for a 4-bed detached. For larger homes, get **LiDAR mapping** so the robot knows where it's been and can clean efficiently in a single pass.

### Do you want mopping too?
If yes, make sure the mop actually works. Cheap combo units just drag a damp cloth — not effective. Models from Roborock and Eufy with vibrating or rotating mop heads actually scrub.

### What's your budget?
- **Under £200:** Eufy RoboVac 11S — basic but functional
- **£200–£450:** Roborock Q-series — proper LiDAR mapping, good value
- **£450–£800:** Eufy X10 Pro Omni — near-flagship with auto-emptying
- **£800+:** Roborock S8 MaxV Ultra — best in class, full automation

## Robot Vacuum FAQ

### How often should a robot vacuum run?
Most homes benefit from daily runs — especially with pets. The beauty of robot vacuums is you set a schedule and forget it. Daily 30-minute runs beat one weekly deep clean for maintaining consistent cleanliness.

### Do robot vacuums work on thick carpet?
Entry-level models (under 2,000Pa) struggle with thick pile. For shaggy rugs or high-pile carpet, you need 4,000Pa+ and ideally a model that can detect carpet and boost suction automatically. The Roomba j9+ and Roborock S8 Max both do this well.

### Can robot vacuums replace a regular vacuum?
For day-to-day maintenance: yes. For deep cleans, corners, stairs, and upholstery: no. Think of a robot vacuum as your daily sweep — it keeps the floors manageable so your full vacuum only needs to come out monthly.

### Is Eufy or Roborock better?
Both are excellent. Roborock generally leads on navigation intelligence and obstacle avoidance. Eufy matches or beats on price for equivalent specs. For 2026, either brand is a safe bet — pick based on the specific model's features vs. your budget.

### Are robot vacuums safe with dogs and cats?
Yes, with some caveats. Most pets get used to robot vacuums quickly (though the first few runs can be chaotic). Models with good obstacle avoidance won't chase or stress your pet. The main risk is pet waste — iRobot's Roomba j9+ has specific pet waste detection; most others don't.

### What is LiDAR in robot vacuums?
LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) fires a laser to build a precise map of your home. Unlike camera-based or gyroscope navigation, LiDAR works in complete darkness and creates accurate room-by-room maps. This lets you set no-go zones, schedule specific rooms, and trust the robot to clean efficiently rather than randomly.
